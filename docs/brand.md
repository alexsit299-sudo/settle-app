# Settle — Brand Guide

## Name
**Settle** — lowercase preferred in product contexts: `settle.`
The period is intentional. It's the personality. Decision made, end of sentence.

## Tagline
**"No more 'idk, what do you want?'"**
Used in marketing, App Store copy, social media. Instantly relatable, shareable.

Secondary tagline (shorter contexts): **"Stop asking. Start eating."**

---

## Voice & Tone
Three words: **Confident. Funny. Impatient.**

Settle is the decisive best friend everyone wishes they had in their group chat.
It never second-guesses itself, roasts slow responders lovingly, and always delivers.

### Sounds like:
- "Let's not do the whole 'idk you pick' thing today."
- "Tyler has been thinking for 6 minutes. We've lit a candle."
- "You're going to Nobu. You're welcome. Stop overthinking it."
- "Majority said no. Nobu it is. Tyler, it'll be fine."

### Never sounds like:
- Corporate: "We've analyzed your preferences to deliver an optimized recommendation"
- Tryhard: "Bestie said Nobu, no cap 💅"
- Passive: "Here is a suggestion based on your inputs"

---

## Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Primary Red | Warm deep red | `#E8294C` |
| Primary Blue | Rich cobalt | `#1D6EE8` |
| Background | Soft off-white | `#F8F8F8` |
| Text | Near black | `#1A1A1A` |
| Accent / Dividers | Light gray | `#E0E0E0` |

### Usage Rules
- **Red** → CTAs, verdicts, energy moments ("SETTLED", "Book Now")
- **Blue** → navigation, trust moments, user actions
- **Off-white** → all backgrounds, keep it light and inviting
- **Never dark mode as default** — Settle is warm and inviting, not moody
- High contrast always — accessibility matters

---

## Typography
- **Style**: Bold, tight sans-serif
- **Recommended fonts**: Inter, DM Sans, or Neue Haas Grotesk
- No script fonts, nothing delicate or decorative
- Headlines: heavy weight, tight tracking
- Body: regular weight, generous line height

---

## Logo
- Wordmark: **settle.** (lowercase, period included)
- The period is the signature — it signals finality and confidence
- App icon: Bold period mark `.` or minimal checkmark in Primary Red on off-white
- Never stretch, recolor, or remove the period

---

## What Settle Is NOT
- Not patriotic (red/white/blue is incidental, not the message)
- Not corporate or enterprise
- Not dark or moody
- Not a utility app — it's a lifestyle brand that happens to solve a problem
