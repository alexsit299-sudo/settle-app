# Settle 🎲
> The app that just answers the question.

**"Where should we eat?"** — Settle ends that conversation in 10 seconds flat.

Settle is an AI-powered group decision app for Millennials and Gen Z. No more 45-minute group chat spirals. No more "idk what do you want." Just a verdict. Delivered fast. With personality.

---

## The Problem
Every week, millions of friend groups waste 30-45 minutes on decisions that should take 30 seconds. The technology to solve this has existed for years. Nobody built it right.

## The Solution
Settle collects everyone's quick inputs, runs them through AI, and delivers a single confident decision — complete with a booking link. Majority rules. No endless debate.

## Target Demographics
- **Primary**: Gen Z (18-29) and Millennials (30-44)
- **Why they care**: Time-poor, decision-fatigued, socially driven
- **Where they are**: iOS, Android, group chats

## How It Works (Food Flow)

1. One person starts a **Decision** — "dinner tonight, 7pm, downtown"
2. Group gets notified — each person taps 3 quick preferences in under 10 seconds
   - Budget: $ / $$ / $$$
   - Vibe: Chill / Fancy / Fast
   - Cuisine: 🍣 🍕 🌮 🍔 Any
3. AI synthesizes inputs + location + ratings + availability
4. Delivers a verdict: **"Carbone, 7:30pm. [Book Now]"**
5. If someone pushes back → majority rules, decision locked
